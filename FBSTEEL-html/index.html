Welcome to FB-STEEL

FB Steel LLC produces grinding steel balls in the Republic of Azerbaijan using the latest forging technologies and European steel material since 2023. Our hardened steel balls meet CIS, European, and global standards, with a surface hardness ranging from 56 to 62 HRC and comply with European steel material 1.2007.

Our

Business

We produce forged grinding steel balls in various chemical compositions and diameters (30 - 125 mm) customized to meet clients needs.

  • Raw material: steel round bars (30 - 125 mm diameter)
  • Uniform heating: induction heating ensures consistent temperature distribution
  • Forging: ensures even density distribution for enhanced durability
  • Heat treatment: advanced techniques achieve optimal hardness with stable temperature control
  • Quality assurance: each steel ball meets the highest standards of performance and reliability
