FB Steel LLC produces grinding steel balls in the Republic of Azerbaijan using the latest forging technologies and European steel material since 2023. Our hardened steel balls meet CIS, European, and global standards, with a surface hardness ranging from 56 to 62 HRC and comply with European steel material 1.2007.




Our

Mission


Here at FB Steel LLC, we combine cutting-edge technologies with a commitment to excellence. As pioneers in grinding steel ball production in Azerbaijan, we strive to boost the country's economic independence by exporting quality products globally.

Our mission is to deliver superior steel solutions while fostering enduring client relationships through exceptional service. We prioritize sustainable practices and employee well-being, aiming to set new benchmarks in the steel industry.

We innovate, expand, and uphold our unwavering dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.




Our


Vision


At FB Steel LLC, we foresee a future where our forged steel products are essential across sectors like construction, automotive, aerospace, and energy. Through continuous research and development, we pioneer solutions that enhance performance, durability, and efficiency.

Sustainability is a core component of our vision. We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint through eco-friendly manufacturing processes and the responsible use of resources. Our goal is to lead the industry in sustainable practices, ensuring that our operations benefit both our customers and the planet.

Driven by innovation, quality, and sustainability, we deliver unmatched value to customers and stakeholders.




Our


Values


Quality: At the heart of everything we do, our rigorous quality control processes ensure that every product meets the highest standards of performance and reliability.

Innovation: Driving our growth and success, we invest in cutting-edge technologies and encourage creative thinking to develop advanced solutions that meet the evolving needs of our industry.

Safety: The safety of our employees, customers, and communities is our top priority. We adhere to strict safety protocols and continuously improve our practices to create a safe working environment.

Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and support, ensuring that our products meet or exceed customer expectations.
